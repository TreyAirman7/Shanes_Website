Hat Size Guide

Find your perfect Kannon fit! Follow our simple steps to measure your head and consult our size chart.

How to Measure Your Head

Getting the right hat size is easy. All you need is a soft measuring tape (like one used for sewing) or a piece of string/ribbon and a ruler.

Wrap the tape around your head as shown.

  1. Wrap the Tape: Place the measuring tape (or string) around your head, about 1/8 inch (0.3 cm) above your eyebrows and ears. Make sure the tape is level and not too tight â€“ it should be snug but comfortable.
  2. Read the Measurement: If using a measuring tape, note the measurement where the tape meets. If using string, mark where the string overlaps, then lay it flat and measure the length with a ruler.
  3. Convert if Necessary: Our sizes are typically listed in centimeters (cm) or inches ("). You may need to convert your measurement. (1 inch = 2.54 cm)

Pro Tip:

If your measurement falls between two sizes, we generally recommend choosing the larger size. You can always add a hat sizer or a bit of foam tape under the sweatband for a snugger fit if needed.
