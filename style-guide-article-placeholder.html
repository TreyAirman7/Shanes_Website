Top 5 Hats for Your Summer Adventures

By Kannon Style Team • Published on May 8, 2025 · Est. 5 min read

Summer is synonymous with sunshine, adventures, and making memories. Whether you're lounging by the pool, exploring a new city, or hiking scenic trails, the right hat is an essential companion. Not only does it offer crucial protection from the sun's rays, but it also adds an instant touch of style to any outfit. At Kannon Hat Co., we've curated our top 5 hat picks to ensure you're covered (literally!) for all your summer escapades.

1. The Classic Wide-Brim Straw Hat

No summer wardrobe is complete without a wide-brim straw hat. Perfect for beach days, garden parties, or simply strolling through a farmers market, this style offers maximum sun protection for your face, neck, and shoulders. Look for tightly woven straw for better UPF rating.

Styling Tip: Pair it with a flowy sundress and espadrilles for a timeless, feminine look. For a more casual vibe, team it with denim shorts and a breezy linen shirt.

2. The Versatile Bucket Hat

The bucket hat has made a major comeback and is a fantastic choice for a relaxed, trendy summer look. Its packable nature makes it ideal for travel, and it comes in a variety of materials from cotton canvas to quick-drying nylon for more active pursuits.

Styling Tip: Rock a canvas bucket hat with your favorite graphic tee and cargo shorts for a street-style edge. For a day by the water, a nylon bucket hat pairs well with swimwear and a rash guard.

3. The Sporty Baseball Cap

A trusty baseball cap is a year-round staple, but it truly shines in the summer. It's perfect for shielding your eyes during outdoor activities, from a morning run to an afternoon baseball game. Opt for breathable materials like cotton or performance fabrics.

"A well-chosen baseball cap can be surprisingly chic. Don't be afraid to pair it with more dressed-up casual pieces for an unexpected twist." - Kannon Style Team

4. The Elegant Panama Hat (or Fedora-Style Straw Hat)

For a touch of sophistication, a Panama hat (or a similar fedora-style straw hat) is an excellent choice. Traditionally handwoven in Ecuador, genuine Panama hats are lightweight, breathable, and incredibly stylish. They can elevate even the simplest summer outfit.

  • Offers excellent sun protection.
  • Lightweight and breathable.
  • Adds a touch of timeless elegance.

5. The Practical Visor

If you prefer to keep your head cool while still protecting your face, a visor is a great option, especially for sports like tennis or golf, or for lounging by the pool when you want to show off your summer hairstyle. Look for visors with a comfortable, adjustable band.

Styling Tip: Pair a sporty visor with your athletic wear or a chic straw visor with your resort wear for a touch of glamour.
