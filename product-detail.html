Product Description

Embrace timeless elegance with the Kannon Classic Wool Fedora. Crafted from 100% premium wool, this hat is designed for both sophisticated style and comfortable wear. The structured crown and medium brim offer a universally flattering silhouette, while the luxurious grosgrain ribbon band adds a touch of classic refinement.

Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or adding a polished finish to your everyday ensemble, this fedora is a versatile wardrobe staple. Its durable construction ensures it will be a cherished piece for years to come.

  • 100% Premium Wool Felt
  • Comfortable interior sweatband
  • Stylish Grosgrain Ribbon Detail
  • Classic Fedora Silhouette
  • Available in multiple colors and sizes

Product Details & Care Instructions

Material:

Outer: 100% Wool Felt

Band: Grosgrain Ribbon

Sweatband: Cotton Blend

Dimensions (Medium Size):

Brim Width: Approx. 2.5 inches (6.35 cm)

Crown Height: Approx. 4.5 inches (11.43 cm)

Care Instructions:
  • Spot clean with a damp cloth only.
  • Use a soft brush to remove dust or lint.
  • Do not wash, bleach, tumble dry, or iron.
  • Store in a cool, dry place, preferably in a hat box to maintain shape.
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or moisture.
