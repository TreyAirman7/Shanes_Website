Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and other major credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely.
How can I track my order?
Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also track your order from your account page if you created one. Or, use our Track Order page.
Can I change or cancel my order after it's placed?
We process orders quickly, but we'll do our best to accommodate changes. Please contact us as soon as possible with your order number. If your order has already been shipped, you may need to follow our returns process.

Shipping

What are your shipping options and costs?
We offer standard and express shipping options. Costs vary based on your location and the selected shipping speed. You can view the shipping costs at checkout before completing your order. For more details, please see our Shipping & Returns Policy.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide! International shipping rates and delivery times will be calculated at checkout. Please note that customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties or taxes.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We want you to love your Kannon hat! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return most items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange, provided they are in new, unworn condition with original tags attached. Please see our full Shipping & Returns Policy for details and any exclusions.
How do I start a return or exchange?
To initiate a return or exchange, please visit our Returns Center page or contact our customer service team with your order number.

Products & Sizing

How do I find my hat size?
Finding the right fit is important! We have a detailed Hat Size Guide that shows you how to measure your head and find the corresponding Kannon hat size. You can find this link on every product page as well.
How should I care for my hat?
Care instructions vary depending on the material of your hat. Generally, we recommend spot cleaning for most hats. Specific care details can often be found on the product page or the hat's inner label. We also have some general tips in our Style Guide.
